Shelter for a family

Families may access emergency shelter through the Virginia Williams Family Resource Center, the central intake for DC families experiencing homelessness. During the public health emergency, intake is conducted by telephone by calling the Shelter Hotline at (202) 399-7093. The shelter hotline is open every day from 8 AM - 12 AM. During hypothermia season (November 1 - April 15), the shelter hotline is open 24 hours a day.
